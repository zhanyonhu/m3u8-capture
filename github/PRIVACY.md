# Privacy — IFD M3U8 Capture

**Last updated:** June 2026

## Summary

This extension helps you find m3u8 links on pages and send selected items to **Internet File Downloader on the same computer**. The maintainer does not operate a cloud service and does not collect your data on a server.

## Local data

Settings and temporary capture data stay in your browser profile on this device.

## Off-device transfer

Data is sent only to software **on your computer**, not to the extension author.
